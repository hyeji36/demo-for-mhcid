GARLIC BUTTER SHRIMP PASTA

This pasta is truly heaven sent. Buttery, garlicky shrimp tossed in a creamy Parmesan white wine sauce,then folded into a bed of angel hair pasta and topped with fresh herbsâ€”and it's all ready in just under 30 minutes. Cooking at home has truly never felt so easy and tasted so good.


Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp. butter, divided
  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. shrimp
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/4 c. dry white wine
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Pinch crushed red pepper flakes
  • 3/4 c. freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for garnish
  • 1 tbsp. freshly chopped parsley
  • 12 oz. cooked angel hair
